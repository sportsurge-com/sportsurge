How to Watch in the United States

Peacock: Your Exclusive Home for SummerSlam

That's right, the only place to legally stream SummerSlam 2025 live in the U.S. is on Peacock. Don't get left out of the action!

  • Platform: Peacock is your one and only ticket to the biggest party of the summer.
  • Subscription: You'll need a Peacock Premium subscription to access all WWE Premium Live Events, including SummerSlam.
  • Devices: Watch on Smart TVs, Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, Chromecast, PlayStation, Xbox, mobile devices, and web browsers.

International Fans: Your Global Guide

Netflix: The New International Home for WWE

In a groundbreaking move, WWE has partnered with Netflix to bring its weekly programming and Premium Live Events to a global audience in many countries, including the UK, Canada, Australia, and more.

  • Subscription: If you have a standard Netflix subscription in one of these regions, you're all set! There are no extra fees.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: How can I watch the SummerSlam 2025 live stream?

In the United States, the only way to watch the live stream is with a Peacock Premium subscription. For most international countries, you can stream it live on Netflix.

Q2: Is SummerSlam 2025 on the WWE Network?

The WWE Network is being phased out in most countries. Most international viewers will need to watch on Netflix. US viewers must use Peacock.

Q3: Can I watch the SummerSlam 2025 live stream for free?

Unfortunately, there is no legal way to watch for free. It requires a subscription to either Peacock (in the US) or Netflix (internationally).

Q4: What time does the SummerSlam 2025 live stream start?

Official start times will be announced closer to the event. Typically, major WWE events have a kickoff show an hour or two before the main card begins.
